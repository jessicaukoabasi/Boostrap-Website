My Hobbies !!

Art

Art has to be one of my top favorite things to do (if not my most favorite thing to do). I've been drawing ever since I was a child, and as I've grown I've learned many tips and technique in sketching and painting. I mostly draw people but I've also grown to enjoy landscape. I really hope to keep improving in the future

Fashion

I have been a fashionista and will continue being a fashionista as long as I live. I feel like my love for art ties in with the way I make outfits. I like to have everything go together just like I'd do with a painting. The form and color and color schemes are very important in both.

Reading

Just like with art, I developed a love for reading at a very young age. Back in middle school, I would plow through up to ten books in a whole month. Nowadays, I'm trying to read more. My favorite genres are science fiction, psychological, and gothic.
